is a sight to behold.

You make all my worries...

and troubles just cease.

to this silly, crazy son,

feels like genuine fun!

I know I talk nonsense...

and bother you a bit,

you never ever quit.

When I’m stuck with a problem...

or feeling down inside,

with arms open wide.

You have an awesome personality...

so special and rare,

I have learned so much...

from the way that you care.

If I ever make mistakes...

please scold me, it’s fine,

I want to learn from you...

and keep staying in line.

You’re extremely the best...

in everything you do,

I hope we stay like this...

forever, me and you.

May your life be filled...

with laughter and glee,

You truly mean...

the whole world to me.

Thank you for the guidance...

for being my light,

Happy Birthday, Dear Inas...

may your future be bright!
